What is the data used for?

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Tracking site visitor numbers and usage patterns
  • Improving site content and user experience
  • Ensuring the technical functionality of the site

The data is not used for advertising, profiling, or sold to third parties.

What data is collected?

Analytics cookies collect anonymous data, such as:

  • Addresses of visited pages
  • Time spent on the site
  • Browser and device type used
  • General geographic location (country/city)
  • Source of arrival to the site (search engine, direct address, etc.)

Cookie management

You can manage cookies in the following ways:

  • Cookie banner: On your first visit to the site, you can accept or decline cookies
  • Browser settings: You can block cookies entirely or delete existing cookies from your browser settings
  • Google Analytics opt-out: You can install the Google opt-out add-on
