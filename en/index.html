About Us

We are a modern, efficient accounting firm offering comprehensive financial management solutions. With our help, you can focus on the core of your business while we take care of routine tasks and your financial matters professionally and efficiently.

Why choose us:

1. Experience: Over 15 years of experience in financial management, from accounts receivable to more demanding financial leadership roles. This gives us a deep understanding of the diverse needs and challenges of different businesses.

2. Versatility: We have worked with a wide range of clients, from hair salons to international IT companies. We master many accounting applications and adopt new ones quickly as needed.

3. Cost-efficiency: We are a highly cost-effective small business, which enables an extremely competitive price-quality ratio. We also have no separate software costs on top of accounting fees. This means we can offer our clients significant savings without compromising on quality of service.

4. Flexibility: You can choose whether you want to handle financial matters entirely remotely or if you need us on-site. We are flexible and adaptable to our clients' needs.

5. Consulting: Our experience in CFO and controller roles enables us to offer broader financial management consulting. We also have solid expertise in tax consulting and resolving tax situations.

Whether it's bookkeeping, tax consulting, or any other financial management need, we are here to help you. Don't hesitate to contact us and let us take care of your accounting so you can focus on growing and developing your business.

Services

-Bookkeeping and financial statements -Tax consulting and tax returns -Budgeting and financial planning -Business formation and changes -Financial statement analysis and reporting -Financial management consulting and development -Business planning and financing

Accounting

Systematic management of your company's financial administration. We take care of recording income and expenses, bank statements and receipts, as well as regulatory filings.

Financial Statements

Financial statement services cover the official reports prepared at the end of the company's fiscal year, including the income statement, balance sheet, and notes. The financial statements comply with the Accounting Act requirements and also include the tax return filing.

Consulting

Expert support in tax consulting and cash flow forecasting, among other areas. We help optimize taxation through legal means and anticipate your company's cash flows so you can make better decisions for developing your business.

Business Planning and Financing

We support your business from planning to applying for financing. We prepare market analysis, business plans, and help find suitable financing channels while preparing application materials.
