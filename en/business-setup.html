What 999 € includes

Five services that together get your business up and running — and keep it going. We work together, you're not alone.

📋

1. Company formation

I help with the practical side: we fill out the PRH forms together, open a bank account, take out the necessary insurances. You sign (the law requires it), I handle everything else.

  • Legal form (Sole trader / Ltd)
  • PRH forms filled together
  • Tax authority and VAT registration
  • Necessary insurances and bank account
⚠️ PRH authority fee: Ltd 300 €, general/limited partnership 300 €, sole trader 75 €, other entrepreneur 400 € — paid directly to PRH.
🎨

2. Brand

We design it together, I handle the technical execution. You're not alone with a designer — we discuss ideas and refine them into a professional look.

  • Logo (2 variations)
  • Colour palette and fonts
  • Business card template
  • Brand guidelines PDF
💻

3. Website (custom-coded)

I code the site from scratch with Claude Code — no WordPress templates. Fully tailored, fast, secure. Hosting on GitHub Pages.

  • Custom-coded, responsive pages
  • Hosting included (GitHub Pages)
  • Google indexing (SEO)
  • Maintenance included in monthly fee
⚠️ Domain (.fi 10–15 €/y, .com 15–20 €/y) invoiced separately once a year. ⚠️ Email with yourcompany.fi requires e.g. Google Workspace ~14 €/month (paid to Google).
📣

4. First ad

We build the first ad and launch offer together. I also teach you how to track results. Ongoing ad management is a separate service (250 €/month).

  • Google or Meta ad
  • Target audience definition
  • Launch offer concept
  • Analytics setup
⚠️ Ad budget 10–50 €/day paid directly to Facebook / Google.
📊

5. Accounting setup

I help you choose the right accounting software and guide you to use it. All options are cloud-based — no installation, just onboarding.

  • Software selection (Easor Mini / Easor / Briox / Procountor / Netvisor)
  • Onboarding and training
  • First month of accounting
  • VAT and Incomes Register filings
⚠️ Software fee (from 5.90 €/month) paid directly to the software provider. ⚠️ Payroll, annual financial statements and other extra work at hourly rate as needed.
